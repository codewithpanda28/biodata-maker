Do I need design skills to create a marriage biodata?

+

No, you don't need any design skills! Our platform is designed to be user-friendly with pre-made templates. You simply need to fill in your information, and our system will create a professionally designed biodata for you.

Can I include a profile photo in the biodata?

+

Yes, all our templates include an option to upload and include your profile photo. We recommend using a recent, clear photo with good lighting and a neutral background for the best impression.

Do I need to register to create a biodata?

+

No registration is required to create a basic biodata. However, creating an account allows you to save your work, access your biodata later for modifications, and enjoy additional premium features.

Can I modify the biodata after downloading?

+

If you have an account with us, you can modify your biodata anytime and download it again. For users without an account, we recommend saving your information before finalizing, as you'll need to recreate the biodata to make changes after downloading.
